Our History

Ashurance Hospitality is a provider of hospitality products and services across various accommodation alternatives and price ranges through its portfolio of brands. We became an independent, pure-play hospitality company on August 1, 2000. Since then, the Company has developed into a well-respected leader in the hospitality industry.
